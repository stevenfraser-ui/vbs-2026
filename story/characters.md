# Character Profiles

## Caleb
Caleb is the steadfast, authoritative leader of the IMF team. He serves as the anchor of the group, maintaining a calm and collected demeanor even when missions go sideways or his team members begin to bicker. More than just a tactical commander, Caleb possesses a strong moral compass grounded in his faith. He views team unity not just as a strategic necessity, but as a spiritual imperative, frequently reminding his team that grace and forgiveness are the only ways to overcome the divisive traps set by their enemies.

Background: A highly seasoned agent, Caleb has survived countless high-stakes operations that would have broken lesser leaders. Over the years, he has witnessed firsthand how pride and internal division can lead to catastrophic failures. This history has shaped his leadership style: he values strict adherence to protocol but knows that true success requires a foundation of absolute trust and love among his team members, even when they make mistakes.

## Cody
Cody is the team’s brilliant, fast-talking tech specialist. He is fiercely confident in his abilities, his algorithms, and his software, often crossing the line into pure arrogance. He firmly believes that everything follows a logical pattern and that his digital solutions are inherently flawless. Consequently, he becomes easily rattled and defensive when his systems are challenged or when his teammates deviate from his meticulously coded plans.

Background: Originally an underground hacker and systems analyst who was used to being the smartest guy in the room, Cody was recruited by the IMF for his unparalleled ability to manipulate networks. Because he spent years working in isolation where his word was law, he struggles with the collaborative aspects of fieldwork. His constant bickering with Elena stems from a deeply ingrained pride and a fear of losing control when variables (like human unpredictability) enter his pristine digital world.

## Malcolm
Malcolm is the seasoned, pragmatic technological counterweight to Cody. Where Cody is hyper-energetic and overly confident in his own algorithms, Malcolm is serious, grounded, and intensely focused on the practical realities of the mission. He is a veteran tech specialist who understands that systems can adapt and foes can be unpredictable. He values strict professionalism and has little patience for reckless improvisation or inflated egos, quickly calling out bad attitudes that could compromise the team.

Background: Drawing on years of high-stakes intelligence experience, Malcolm has seen enough operations go wrong to know that discipline is just as important as technical skill. He is deeply loyal to Caleb and the IMF, but this loyalty makes him fiercely protective of the mission's integrity. Because he holds himself and his teammates to such a high professional standard, he can struggle with extending grace; when a teammate makes a critical mistake, his instinct is to hold onto the offense rather than quickly forgive.

## Elena
Elena is a sharp-witted, fiercely pragmatic, and highly capable field agent. She has little patience for incompetence or oversized egos, making her the natural foil to Cody. When a plan starts to fall apart, her first instinct is to take immediate, unilateral action to fix it. While she advocates for structure and discipline, her tendency to adapt on the fly without communicating her changes often creates new vulnerabilities for the team.

Background: Trained in deep-cover intelligence and high-risk infiltration, Elena is used to operating in environments where a split-second decision means life or death. She learned early on to rely primarily on her own instincts rather than trusting support teams, leading to a fiercely independent streak. Her character arc requires her to lower her guard, admit her own mistakes, and learn that true strength in the IMF comes from vulnerability and trusting her teammates.

## Derek Hunt
Derek is a former Marine Special Forces operative newly assigned to the IMF. Possessing a resume that boasts top-of-the-class honors in tactical operations and counter-intelligence, he is undeniably skilled. However, he masks his lethal capabilities with a disarmingly casual and breezy attitude—bringing juice boxes and trail mix to top-secret briefings. He is eager, highly confident, and believes his ability to improvise is his greatest asset.

Background: Because Derek has always been a prodigy, breezing through elite military training with natural talent and charm, he has rarely had to face the real-world consequences of his own rule-breaking. He views protocols as mere suggestions and thinks "innovating" on the fly is what makes a great agent. This overconfidence eventually catches up to him when his failure to follow instructions ruins a critical mission step, leading directly to his capture by the Null.

## The Null
The Null is a mysterious, unseen antagonist who operates masterfully in the shadows. He is a formidable hacker and thief, but his true danger lies in his psychological warfare. He understands that a united team is almost impossible to defeat in a straightforward fight, so he meticulously targets the emotional and interpersonal cracks within the IMF—specifically their pride, their unwillingness to forgive, and their quickness to assign blame.

Background: Little is known about the Null's origins, though it is clear he possesses extensive knowledge of IMF protocols, facility layouts, and human psychology. He seems to view human connection as a predictable set of weaknesses to be exploited. He relishes the opportunity to not just defeat his enemies, but to watch them tear themselves apart from the inside, believing that failure and division are the natural states of humanity.

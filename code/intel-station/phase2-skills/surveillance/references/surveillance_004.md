## Objective

Conduct detailed satellite imagery analysis of the facility identified as TITAN SYSTEMS' classified compound "The Vault" (coordinates confirmed via EA-718).

## Satellite Imagery Analysis

### Compound Overview

The Vault compound covers approximately **12 acres** of land in a remote, sparsely populated area. The surrounding terrain has been cleared for 2 kilometers in every direction — creating a massive buffer zone with no civilian structures, roads, or vegetation that could provide concealment for approaching personnel.

### Perimeter Security (Visible from Orbit)

| Layer | Description |
|---|---|
| **Outer Ring** | Electrified fence (8 feet) with razor wire. Motion sensor posts every 50 meters. |
| **Middle Ring** | Concrete barrier wall (12 feet). Vehicle checkpoints at 2 access points only. |
| **Inner Ring** | Chain-link fence with camera arrays. Foot patrol observed every 15 minutes. |
| **Guard Towers** | 4 elevated positions at compound corners. Staffed 24/7. |

### Main Structure

- **Above Ground:** 6-story building, rectangular footprint (~80m x 60m)
- **Construction Material:** Reinforced concrete with signal-dampening exterior coating
- **Roof Features:** Satellite dishes (3), HVAC units (12+), solar panel arrays covering ~40% of roof surface
- **Vehicle Access:** Underground garage entrance on north side

### Thermal Imaging Results

- **Below Ground Levels:** Thermal imaging confirms **at minimum 3 underground levels** extending beneath the main structure
- **Heat Signature:** The hottest zone is centered 3 stories below ground — consistent with a dense computing environment requiring active cooling
- **The Server Core:** A concentrated heat source approximately 40m x 40m is visible at the center of the lowest detected underground level. This matches the dimensions described in the leaked Vault assessment (FR-TITAN-CLASSIFIED).

### Supporting Infrastructure (On-Site)

| Infrastructure | Description |
|---|---|
| **Power Substation** | Dedicated electrical substation on the east side of the compound. Connected to regional grid via high-voltage transmission lines. |
| **Solar Array** | Ground-mounted solar panels covering ~3 acres south of the main building. |
| **Backup Generators** | 4 large diesel generators visible in a hardened shelter on the west side. |
| **Cooling Infrastructure** | Large cooling towers visible on the north side. Thermal output consistent with industrial-scale liquid cooling. |

## Assessment

The Vault is one of the most heavily secured and purpose-built facilities we have ever observed. Every element of its design points to a single purpose: keeping whatever is inside running at maximum capacity while preventing unauthorized access.

The concentrated thermal signature at the underground center of the facility is consistent with the **Server Core** — the most likely physical location of The Light.

**Any mission targeting this facility will face extraordinary security challenges.** Detailed security protocol analysis is required before any operational planning can proceed.

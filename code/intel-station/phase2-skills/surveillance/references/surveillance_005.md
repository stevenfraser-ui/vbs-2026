## Objective

Monitor and analyze transport traffic to and from the facility identified as TITAN SYSTEMS' classified compound "The Vault."

## Observation Period

Continuous surveillance conducted over [REDACTED] days from multiple concealed positions outside The Vault's buffer zone.

## Findings

### Regular Traffic Patterns

| Traffic Type | Frequency | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Staff Vehicles** | Daily, 06:00 & 18:00 | ~40-50 vehicles per shift change via monitored road. All vehicles registered to TITAN SYSTEMS subsidiary companies. |
| **Supply Trucks** | 2-3 per week | Standard commercial trucks delivering food, equipment, and supplies. Inspected at outer checkpoint. |
| **Fuel Tankers** | Weekly | Diesel delivery for backup generators. |

### High-Security Convoys

On **3 separate occasions** during the observation period, high-security convoys were observed:

| Convoy | Composition | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Convoy 1** | 2 armored SUVs, 1 armored truck, 1 signal-jamming vehicle | Arrived at 02:00. Signal jamming active — all surveillance equipment within 5km went dark for 45 minutes. Departed at 04:30. |
| **Convoy 2** | 3 armored SUVs, electronic countermeasure escort | Arrived at 03:00. Carried what appeared to be large server racks (thermal signature visible through vehicle walls). Departed empty at 05:00. |
| **Convoy 3** | 2 armored SUVs, 1 unmarked sedan | Arrived at 01:00. Single passenger exited the sedan and was escorted directly inside. Departed next morning. |

### Convoy Analysis

- All high-security convoys arrived during **nighttime hours** (01:00 - 04:00), minimizing potential civilian observation
- Convoy dates correlate with known Light transfer events and major system upgrades
- Signal-jamming capability indicates military-grade operational security
- **Security posture at this facility exceeds anything we've observed at standard TITAN SYSTEMS locations**

### Notable Observation

During Convoy 2, our thermal imaging equipment briefly captured the cargo before signal jamming activated. The truck contained what appeared to be **custom-built server racks** — dimensions and configuration unlike any commercial hardware. These were likely purpose-built for the Server Core.

## Assessment

Transport traffic confirms The Vault is an **active, high-priority facility** receiving ongoing infrastructure upgrades. The nighttime convoy schedule and signal-jamming capability demonstrate that TITAN SYSTEMS is taking extraordinary measures to protect this location.

The ongoing delivery of new server hardware is consistent with The Light's exponential growth — the computing infrastructure is being continuously expanded to keep pace with its demands.

## Objective

Analyze encrypted data burst patterns from the unidentified source tagged as **UNKNOWN-7**.

## Findings

- UNKNOWN-7 has transmitted **47 encrypted data bursts** over the past 90 days.
- Transmission frequency is **increasing** — 8 in the first month, 15 in the second, 24 in the most recent month.
- Data bursts are being sent to **multiple endpoints** across at least 9 countries.
- The pattern matches what analysts call a **pre-deployment communication cycle** — increased activity typically seen when a major project nears completion.
- Each burst contains significant amounts of data — far more than text messages. Analysis suggests UNKNOWN-7 may be transmitting **code or software packages**.

## Assessment

UNKNOWN-7 is likely preparing for a deployment or launch of some kind. The volume and frequency of data transmissions, combined with their software-like size, suggests UNKNOWN-7 is distributing or testing components of a large digital project.

# HOSTILE ORGANIZATION PROFILE — IRON VEIL
**Classification:** TOP SECRET  
**Prepared by:** Counter-Intelligence Division  
**Last Updated:** [REDACTED]  
**Reference:** HOP-303  

---

## Overview

**IRON VEIL** is a state-sponsored intelligence group operating under the authority of [REDACTED]. They maintain extensive signals intelligence operations and are considered one of the most sophisticated hostile actors currently pursuing The Light.

## Known Activities

- Monitoring encrypted communications from multiple sources, including the signal source we've tagged as UNKNOWN-7
- Intercepts show they are *"monitoring traffic from someone they call 'the designer'"*
- They appear to have more intelligence about The Light's creator than other hostile groups
- One IRON VEIL internal memo reads: *"The designer is the key. Find the designer, control The Light."*

## Assessment of Threat Level

**VERY HIGH** — IRON VEIL is the closest of any hostile group to understanding both what The Light is AND who created it. They have connected "the designer" to the UNKNOWN-7 signal source, though it is unclear if they know the code name LOGOS.

## Known Capabilities

- State-level signals intelligence (SIGINT) infrastructure
- Satellite surveillance network
- Extensive human intelligence (HUMINT) network
- Ability to deploy field teams to any country within 24 hours

## Key Note

IRON VEIL's intelligence apparatus uses the term "the designer" rather than LOGOS. This may mean they haven't decoded the code-name registry, or they have their own designation for this individual.

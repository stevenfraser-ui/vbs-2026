## Summary

Agent embedded in Eastern European black market network for 6 weeks. Primary objective: confirm existence and nature of "The Light."

## Findings

- Multiple black market contacts confirmed The Light is real and "incredibly valuable."
- One contact (arms dealer, codename VIPER) claimed The Light was being offered for private sale: *"This thing could change the balance of power overnight. Governments would pay anything."*
- VIPER implied The Light was a physical object — possibly a weapon or energy source.
- Another contact (tech broker) laughed when VIPER said it was a weapon, said: *"VIPER doesn't know what he's talking about. He thinks everything valuable fits in a suitcase."*

## Assessment

Conflicting intelligence. VIPER's description suggests a physical weapon or power source. However, the tech broker's reaction suggests VIPER may be wrong about The Light's nature. The tech broker refused to elaborate further before disappearing from the network.

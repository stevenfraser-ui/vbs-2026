## Summary

Surveillance operation targeting a known arms dealer suspected of brokering a deal involving The Light.

## Findings

- Subject observed meeting with three unidentified individuals at a private restaurant.
- Audio surveillance captured the following fragments:
  - Subject: *"Everyone wants The Light, but nobody really knows what it is."*
  - Individual #2: *"I was told it's a weapon. Something new. Something dangerous."*
  - Subject: *"That's what they ALL say. But I've dealt in weapons my whole life, and this... this is something different. I'm not even sure it's something you can hold."*
- Subject was visibly confused and frustrated. He later told his driver: *"I don't know what I'm selling anymore."*

## Assessment

Strong indication that The Light is NOT a conventional weapon. The subject's confusion suggests it may not be a physical object at all. The phrase "not something you can hold" is particularly notable.

## Recommendation

Shift investigation away from arms networks. Focus on technology and digital intelligence channels.

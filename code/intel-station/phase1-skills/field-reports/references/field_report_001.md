## Summary

Team deployed to [REDACTED] following intelligence suggesting "The Light" was being transported via private convoy.

## Findings

- Convoy intercepted. No physical payload found — vehicles carried only encrypted hard drives and networking equipment.
- Recovered partial data from one drive before self-destruct protocol activated. Data fragment reads: *"...a device of immense value... the culmination of years of work..."*
- No physical description of The Light was found in any recovered documents.
- One driver, when questioned, stated: "You'll never find it. You don't even know what you're looking for."

## Assessment

We still do not know what The Light looks like or how it is transported. The reference to "device" may be misleading — the presence of computing equipment at the convoy site is unusual for a physical weapons transfer.

## Recommendation

Cross-reference with Operation GLACIER and Operation DUSTLINE reports. More data needed.

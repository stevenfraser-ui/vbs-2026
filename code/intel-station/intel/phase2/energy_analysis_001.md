## Objective

Conduct a global satellite thermal imaging survey to identify anomalous power consumption signatures that may indicate The Light's current location. Since The Light is software requiring massive computational resources, its host facility should produce a distinctive thermal and energy signature visible from orbit.

## Methodology

Using infrared and thermal imaging satellites, we scanned for energy consumption anomalies exceeding 100 MW in locations not associated with known industrial, military, or power-generation facilities.

## Results

Three significant anomalous signatures detected worldwide:

### Anomaly 1 — Eastern Europe
- **Estimated Power Draw:** 110 MW
- **Location:** Industrial zone, [REDACTED]
- **Identification:** Steel manufacturing complex. **Confirmed NOT related** to The Light. Thermal signature consistent with blast furnace operations.
- **Status:** ELIMINATED

### Anomaly 2 — Central Asia  
- **Estimated Power Draw:** 130 MW
- **Location:** Rural area, [REDACTED]
- **Identification:** Large-scale cryptocurrency mining operation. **Confirmed NOT related** to The Light. Facility is publicly registered and audited.
- **Status:** ELIMINATED

### Anomaly 3 — [REDACTED]
- **Estimated Power Draw:** ~200 MW  
- **Location:** Coordinates [REDACTED]
- **Identification:** **UNKNOWN.** No industrial facility, military base, or power plant is registered at this location. The area is officially designated as "undeveloped land" in government records.
- **Status:** ⚠️ **REQUIRES FURTHER ANALYSIS**

## Assessment

Anomaly 3 is extraordinary. Key observations:

1. **200 MW** exceeds any known commercial data center by a factor of four
2. The location is officially "empty" — no registered facility exists there
3. Thermal pattern is consistent with **computing infrastructure** (steady, sustained heat), not industrial processes (variable, episodic heat)
4. The power draw has been **increasing steadily** over the past 18 months — matching The Light's known exponential growth pattern

**Recommendation:** Conduct focused analysis of Anomaly 3. Cross-reference location with known corporate facility databases, particularly TITAN SYSTEMS (see CI-519).

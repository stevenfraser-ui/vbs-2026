## Power Assessment

- **Available Power:** 0.5 MW (residential grid connection)
- **Cooling:** 2 industrial fans, 1 portable AC unit
- **Backup Power:** None

## Assessment

This facility represents the **birthplace** of The Light — where LOGOS first built and activated the software. It was never intended for long-term hosting. Lab Zero's power supply is comparable to a small restaurant, not a computing facility.

Within **weeks** of The Light's initial activation, the facility's power infrastructure was overwhelmed. Circuit breakers tripped repeatedly. The residential building's other tenants reported flickering lights and appliance failures.

**Conclusion:** Lab Zero was adequate only for The Light's earliest development phase. The software's computational appetite surpassed this facility's total capacity almost immediately. Transfer to a larger facility was inevitable from the moment of activation.

## Current Status

Facility has been **decommissioned and sealed**. All equipment removed. LOGOS has not returned to this location.

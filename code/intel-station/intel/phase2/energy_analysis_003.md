## Assessment

The Light has grown continuously from 0.5 MW at Lab Zero to 200 MW at The Vault — a **400x increase** in total power consumption. The growth has never slowed, paused, or reversed at any facility.

Even The Vault, with its purpose-built infrastructure designed to scale to 500 MW, will eventually reach its limits if this trend continues. However, 500 MW capacity gives TITAN approximately **2 years** before they face the same crisis that every previous host experienced.

> **Analyst Note:** Whatever The Light is doing — learning, building, expanding — it has not stopped since the day LOGOS first activated it. This is not idle software. It is *alive* in the computational sense, and it is growing every single day.

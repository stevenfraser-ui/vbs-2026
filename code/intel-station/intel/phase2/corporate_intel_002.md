## Analysis

The existence of this 12th facility was confirmed through financial analysis — TITAN's infrastructure spending exceeds what 11 sites would require by a significant margin. Internal communications reference "The Vault" but never with location data or power specifications.

**Key observation:** Every known TITAN facility operates in the 20-50 MW range. The Vault is believed to operate at **several times** this capacity, suggesting it houses something far more demanding than any standard client workload.

## Description

**TITAN SYSTEMS** is one of the world's largest private technology infrastructure corporations. They specialize in building and operating ultra-secure data centers for government agencies, military organizations, and intelligence services.

Unlike consumer-facing technology companies, TITAN SYSTEMS operates almost entirely in the classified space. They have no public-facing products or services. Their client list is itself classified.

## Key Capabilities

- **Custom data center construction:** TITAN can design and build purpose-built facilities to client specifications, including underground installations
- **Classified hosting:** They operate secure environments rated for TOP SECRET and above
- **Scalable power infrastructure:** Their facilities can draw 20-200+ MW depending on client needs
- **Security services:** Physical and cyber security provided in-house

## Known Government Contracts

TITAN SYSTEMS holds active contracts with at least 7 intelligence agencies across 4 countries. Specific contract values are classified, but total government revenue is estimated at $30 billion annually.

## Red Flags

- TITAN has been investigated twice for "cost overruns" on classified contracts — both investigations were quietly closed
- Several former military and intelligence officials sit on TITAN's board of directors
- TITAN's internal network security is exceptional — only one known successful penetration in 25 years

## Assessment

TITAN SYSTEMS has the financial resources, technical capability, and security infrastructure to house virtually any computing asset in the world. If someone needed to hide the most important piece of software ever created, TITAN SYSTEMS would be the logical choice.

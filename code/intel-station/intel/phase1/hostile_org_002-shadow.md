# HOSTILE ORGANIZATION PROFILE — SHADOW COLLECTIVE
**Classification:** SECRET  
**Prepared by:** Cyber Intelligence Division  
**Last Updated:** [REDACTED]

---

## Overview

The **SHADOW COLLECTIVE** is an elite hacker-for-hire group with no known national affiliation. They specialize in cyber espionage, data theft, and digital infiltration. They are among the few hostile groups that understand The Light may be digital in nature.

## Known Activities

- Executed over 200 confirmed cyber intrusions against corporate and government targets
- Rumored to hold a contract to "acquire exclusive digital intellectual property of extraordinary value" — almost certainly referring to The Light
- Have been probing server networks and data centers worldwide, looking for unusual digital signatures
- One intercepted SHADOW communication reads: *"It's not in a building. It's on a server. We just need to find which one."*

## Assessment of Threat Level

**VERY HIGH** — Unlike MERIDIAN GROUP, SHADOW COLLECTIVE correctly understands that The Light is digital. Their technical capabilities make them the most dangerous group actively pursuing The Light. They have the skills to find and steal software.

## Known Capabilities

- Advanced penetration testing and network intrusion
- Custom encryption-breaking tools
- AI-assisted code analysis
- Distributed attack infrastructure across 30+ countries

## Key Intelligence Gap

SHADOW COLLECTIVE knows The Light is digital but does not appear to know who created it. They have not been observed using the code name LOGOS or referencing "the designer."

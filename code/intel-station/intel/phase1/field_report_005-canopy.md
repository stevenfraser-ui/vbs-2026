## Findings

- *"Multiple governments are interested in The Light. Not just ours — at least twelve that I know of."*

## Assessment

Source is considered reliable (cross-referenced with existing intelligence). Key takeaway: The Light's creator is central to understanding what it is. Source strongly implies previous assumptions about its physical nature are all wrong.

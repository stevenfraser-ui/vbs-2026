## Summary

Strike team deployed to a suspected holding site for The Light in [REDACTED]. This was our closest approach to date.

## Findings

- Team breached the outer perimeter of a heavily guarded facility.
- Upon entering the primary target zone, team found **NO physical device, weapon, or object of any kind.**
- What was found:
  - **Rows of high-powered computer servers** filling the entire ground floor
  - **Multiple cooling systems** running at maximum capacity
  - **Fiber optic cables** leading to an underground data center
  - A single terminal displaying the message: *"TRANSFER COMPLETE. ASSET RELOCATED."*
- The facility's power draw was enormous — estimated equivalent to a small city.
- Whatever was stored here was **digital**, not physical.

## Assessment

**CRITICAL:** This is the strongest evidence yet that The Light is not a physical object. The massive computing infrastructure at this site suggests The Light exists in digital form. It was stored on these servers and was moved electronically before our team arrived.

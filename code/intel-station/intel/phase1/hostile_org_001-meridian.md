## Overview

The **MERIDIAN GROUP** is an international arms brokering network with operations across Europe, Asia, and the Middle East. Originally focused on conventional weapons trafficking, they have recently pivoted to pursuing high-value technology assets.

## Known Activities

- Brokered illegal weapons deals worth an estimated $2.3 billion over the past decade
- Recently pivoted interest from physical weapons to "emerging tech" — specifically The Light
- Multiple operatives spotted near major data centers in three countries
- Attempted to purchase information about The Light on black market networks

## Assessment of Threat Level

**HIGH** — MERIDIAN has the resources and network to be a serious threat, but their approach suggests they fundamentally misunderstand The Light. Their operatives continue to search for a physical object, conduct "shipping" negotiations, and plan "extraction" operations. This indicates they have NOT learned that The Light is likely digital.

## Known Personnel

- Leader: [REDACTED], known only by alias "THE BROKER"
- At least 40 field operatives across 12 countries
- Connected to arms dealers, corrupt officials, and shipping companies

## Key Intelligence Gap

MERIDIAN references "the designer" in multiple intercepted communications but does not appear to know who this person is or what code name they use.

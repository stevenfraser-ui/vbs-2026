**FROM:** Director of Physical Security, PROMETHEUS DYNAMICS
**TO:** Chief Executive Officer, PROMETHEUS DYNAMICS

**SUBJECT:** Initiation of Project AEGIS — Facility S-7 Security Overhaul

---

## 1. PURPOSE

This memorandum formally initiates **Project AEGIS** — the comprehensive security overhaul for **Facility S-7** (Svalbard). Given the critical nature of the asset housed at S-7, current perimeter and access controls are insufficient. The board has authorized a $47M budget for the installation of multi-layered physical and digital security systems.

## 2. COMPARTMENTALIZATION DIRECTIVE

Due to the extreme sensitivity of the asset, **no single contractor or vendor shall have knowledge of the complete security architecture.** All construction and installation will be managed through **compartmentalized contractor engagement**:

- Each security layer will be contracted to a **separate, independent firm**
- Contractors will operate on **staggered schedules** — no two teams on-site simultaneously
- All contracts will be routed through an **intermediary procurement entity** to obscure the connection to PROMETHEUS DYNAMICS
- Contractor personnel will sign **enhanced NDAs** with liquidated damages clauses

## 3. SECURITY LAYERS

Three distinct security systems will be installed:

| Layer | Function | Status |
|-------|----------|--------|
| **Layer 1** | Kinetic countermeasures — physical access denial | Contractor TBD |
| **Layer 2** | Adaptive access systems — dynamic authorization | Contractor TBD |
| **Layer 3** | Autonomous threat response — automated containment | Contractor TBD |

## 4. TIMELINE

- **Q2 2024:** Contractor selection and contract execution
- **Q3 2024:** Layer 1 installation
- **Q4 2024:** Layers 2 and 3 installation
- **Q1 2025:** Integration testing and certification

## 5. RISK NOTE

If any single contractor were to obtain knowledge of all three layers, they would possess the complete blueprint needed to bypass Facility S-7 security. **This must not happen.**
